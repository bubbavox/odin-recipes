Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

Description

A classic take on everyone's favorite classic favorite.

Ingredients

  • peanuts
  • butter
  • jelly, jam, or preserves
  • empty sandwich

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. Combine peanuts, butter, and fruit in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add sandwich
  4. Enjoy!
