Strawberry Rhubarb Crisp

Based on a recipe by Michelle Davis, 2024 (Allrecipes)

Description

This highly addictive treat tempers the savage bite of rhubarb with sweet juicy strawberries, and enough sugar to put an elephant to sleep. A crisp oat topping ensures that the drooling masses will keep coming back for more.
